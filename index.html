Laduni.app

Laduni is a web app online learning platform and marketplace offering a learning solution for students and working adults. It is a platform that helps users find ways to learn their subject of choice while matching them with the right mentor or instructor. Laduni also offers users the opportunity to share their knowledge, expertise or passionas an expert or part-time tutor through the platform. Should users choose to teach online, Laduni gives them total flexibility to decide their hourly fees and viability. Learners can purchase credit to enrol in the subjects of choice and choose potential tutors or instructors to supervise them. Can be assessed at https://dev.laduni.app
